Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to answer your questions and put your mind at ease.

How long after an accident can I seek treatment?

It's crucial to seek care as soon as possible. Many injuries, especially whiplash, may not show symptoms for days or even weeks. Timely treatment can prevent long-term complications and is often required for insurance claims.

Do you handle the insurance and legal paperwork for me?

Yes, we do. Our team specializes in car accident cases and will handle all the necessary insurance and legal documentation. This allows you to focus on what matters most: your recovery.

Will my car insurance cover the treatment?

In most cases, yes. We work directly with car insurance companies to ensure your treatment is covered. We offer a free consultation to verify your benefits and discuss your specific situation without any obligation.

What if my pain is minor or hasn't started yet?

Even minor discomfort or no symptoms at all should be evaluated. A thorough examination can detect hidden injuries that could lead to chronic pain if left untreated. Don't wait for the pain to get worse.
